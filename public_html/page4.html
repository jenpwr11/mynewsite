Referral Program

                     Power Washing Referral Program That Puts $ In Your Pocket

Send Us Customers And Earn Credit toward your next wash project

Our company strives on building repeat business with our customers because you are the reason we stay in business. We have no problem rewarding you for bringing us new customers to build relationships with, and in fact we encourage it.

Here’s how it works....you give us up to three names and addresses of people you know, whom might be interested in our services, and we will keep this information on file for six months. If someone you referred hires us within the six month period, and they mention your name, we will send you a coupon for 10% of the money for their first time service. This coupon can be used toward your next Power Washing Service Example: Your referral hires us to wash his/her house for $350 then we send you $35 Coupon - it’s that simple! There is no limit to the amount of money you can make.

What we won’t do with the referrals

We won’t call or harass them.

We won’t sell or give their name to any other company

We will only send them our usual advertising, it will be up to them to call us.

Rules You Must Follow

You must be an existing customer to qualify.

Your referral must mention your name when they hire us.

Referral must be within our service area (50 mile radius around Northampton)
