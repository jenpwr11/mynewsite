How high can the Gibbon Gutter Cleaner reach without the extension kit. It says single story but the video uses a pretty low roof line Also will it hook up to the 2 stroke Toro 25 Blower/Vac ?
Rod 
from Michigan 
November 08, 2016
Answer
The Gibbon unit can reach a standard height of about 8 feet. Depending on ones height, someone may be able to reach higher, but 8 feet would be standard without the extension kit.

In regards to the compatibility, it will require 260 CFM or more, with an outlet pipe size from 2.5" to 3.3".
